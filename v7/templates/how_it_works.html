How It Works

This application allows users to upload time series data and generate forecasts using pre-trained Large Language Models (LLMs).

You can upload your dataset in various formats (CSV, JSON, XML, Excel), specify the target and time fields, and choose from a selection of Chronos models. The application then processes your input, slices it into windows, and applies a forecasting model to generate predictions.

The results are visualized through an embedded Grafana dashboard, where you can explore the predicted trends and compare them against your historical data.

You can also slide through time windows using the navigation buttons provided, enabling a step-by-step exploration of how forecasts evolve.
